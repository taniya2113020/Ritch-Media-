Frequently asked questions

What size should my presentation slides be?

Most presentations use a narrow widescreen size (16:9), which is 1920px by 1080px. Standard slides (4:3) measure at 1024px x 768px. Choose whichever format you prefer and use the Resize tool to change the slide size at any time.

What kind of presentations can I make?

Create sales presentations, webinar presentations, marketing and pitch decks, keynotes, school projects, tutorials, or even presentations just for fun. There are endless creative opportunities.
